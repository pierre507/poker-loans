Privacy Policy Effective Date: May 19, 2026 Poker MGR ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by City Vibes. This policy describes how we handle your information. Information We Collect When you create an account, we collect your email address and password for authentication purposes. The app stores your poker session data, bankroll records, and loan/debt information that you voluntarily enter. How We Use Your Information Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality: tracking your poker sessions, bankroll, loans, and debts. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data Storage Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data. Session presets and custom field values are stored locally on your device. Third-Party Services The app uses the following third-party services: Supabase — for authentication and data storage. CoinGecko API — for cryptocurrency price data (no personal data is sent). Open Exchange Rates API — for currency conversion rates. Data Retention Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Security We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and row-level database security policies to protect your data. Children's Privacy This app is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
